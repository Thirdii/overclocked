Main Components

Processor

The computer processor (or CPU) is like the brain of your device, handling all the thinking and calculations. It's the reason your computer can process info and run programs smoothly. It's like when humans think. they're using their brains, in this case, the computer is using the processor to think. In a nutshell, it's the powerhouse that makes your tech tick.

Motherboard

Think of it like a cyber house for computers, that's the motherboard. It's where all the important stuff connects. It's like the motherboard's job is to throw a party and invite all the components—CPU, memory, storage—to mingle and collaborate. Without it, your computer parts would just be loners with no place to hang out.

Memory

Computer memory is where your device stores information for quick access. It's like a digital notepad that keeps important stuff handy for the computer to use. Whether it's your favorite game or a document you're working on, it's stored in the memory for easy retrieval when needed. The bigger the memory is, the more data it can run.

Main Peripherals

Monitor

A computer monitor is basically your computer's TV screen. It shows you all the cool stuff your computer is doing, from watching videos to playing games. It's like the window to your digital world. These monitors come in different sizes, with each size having a dedicated resolution for it. So, pick your monitors wisely if you really want to be immersed in the digital landscape of what your computer offers.

Keyboard

A keyboard is like a typing machine for your computer. You press keys to tell the computer what to do. It's the tool that helps you talk to your computer in words. Keyboards are super customizable too. You can put different colored keycaps, change key switches to have a different sound when you press them keys, and similar with monitors, they come in all kinds of sizes too. Each size being represented by their key percentage (%) which tells you the amount of keys on a keyboard.

Mouse

Don't worry, this mouse doesn't knack on your left over food. A computer mouse is like a little hand for your computer. You move it around to point at things on the screen and click to make stuff happen. It's the go-to gadget for navigating and bossing your computer around. Usually, mouses have 3 buttons (left button, right button, and the scroll wheel) but they also can be bought with numerous mouse buttons on the side giving you that extra control.

Processor

A processor, also known as a CPU (Central Processing Unit), is like the brain of your computer or phone. It's the boss that tells all the other parts what to do. It crunches numbers super fast, making your apps and games run smoothly. Think of it as the engine revving up your tech and making it wicked fast! Currently, there are two brands of processors to choose from. Pick one that suits you best.

Graphics Card

If the processor is the brains of your computer, the graphics card is like the heart of your computer, especially when it comes to visual data. It's the part that makes sure all the cool images, videos, and games on your screen run smoothly and properly rendered. Think of it as a powerful artist that turns data into awesome pictures for you to enjoy.
